Staples Home Office

The objective for this project was to create an interactive mobile application in which any person or company can access Staples' new line of furniture, personalize and preview them in a real or 3D virtual environment, and finally purchase them for delivery or local store pick-up. This application would be available via smartphones, tablets and at Staples indoor kiosk. To aid with navigating seammlessly through the entire application are floating menus, product comparison options, personalisation and help widgets and a linear completion map to indicate where in the entire shopping experience you are.
